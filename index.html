Select any highlighted area on the map to open a detail panel. You can also search by booth number, pack name, feature name, or any notes you add to the data block in the script.

  • A1-A18 and D1-D18 are generated as individual booths.
  • B1-B16 and C1-C18 are generated as center-row booths.
  • Event features such as Registration, VIP Area, Tracks, Stage, First Aid, and Loading are also interactive.
